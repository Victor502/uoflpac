U ofL PAC

The University of Louisville has built, among fans and teams, a culture of hard work and winning. This was built by a rock solid group of leaders at the top, with a vision. This vision has, of course, brought us victory in athletics, increased enrollment, and pride for our city. They also transformed the campus and other facilities, as well as opened doors to the ACC for academics. To look around at the leadership in place right now, you'll struggle to find any of the familiar faces that truly built this.

To paraphrase a quote that is reminiscent of our situation— if you tear something down from the outside it can be rebuilt, possibly even stronger. But, if you tear something down from within, it may never rebuild again.

To this end, we have formed the University of Louisville Protection and Advocacy Coalition. UofL PAC is an active group of citizens, students, and alumni keeping watch. Our mission is to protect the University, its well-renowned programs, and the investments the citizens of this city have made in them, from threats — both internal and external.
